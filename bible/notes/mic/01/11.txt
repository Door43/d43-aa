===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Pass by, inhabitants of Shaphir, in nakedness and shame**  - The name of this city means "pleasing." AT: "The people of Shaphir were pleasing. Now they walk by naked and ashamed." (See: [[:aa:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names|Translate Names]])
  * **in nakedness and shame** - Enemy armies made their prisoners walk naked. (See: [[:aa:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
  * **Zaanan** - The name of this city means "go out."
  * **Beth Ezel** - The name of this city means "house beside another" or "helper."
  * **for their protection is taken away** - AT: "for I have taken away everything that might protect them" (See: [[:aa:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
  * **Maroth** - The name of this city means "bitterness."
