===== Translation Notes: =====

    * **third day**  - "day number three" (See: Biblical Numbers)
    * **whatever meat of the sacrifice remains....must be burned** - "You must burn any leftover sacrificial meat" (See: Active - Passive)
    * **if any of the meat.....is eaten** - AT: "if you eat any of the meat" (See: Active - Passive)
    * **it will not be accepted** - AT: "God will not accept it' (See: Active - Passive)
    * **neither will it be credited to the one who offered it" - "the one who offered it will have no credit" (See: Active - Passive)
    * **disgusting thing** - "a thing which disgusts" This adjective comes from the verb 'to disgust'. (See: Verbal Forms)
