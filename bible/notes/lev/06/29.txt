====== Leviticus 06:29-30 ======

===== TFT: =====

<usfm> \v 29 Any male in a priest's family may eat some of the cooked meat; that meat is very holy. \v 30 But if the blood of those offerings is brought into the Sacred Tent to enable the people to be forgiven for having sinned, the meat of those animals must not be eaten. The meat must be completely burned." </usfm>
