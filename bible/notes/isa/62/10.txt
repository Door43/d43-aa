===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Come through, come through the gates!** - The phrase "come through" is repeated to emphasize that the people of Israel must leave the other nations and come back to the land of Israel.
  * **Build it, build  the highway** - The word "build" is repeated to emphasize that God wants the road prepared so the people can come to him.
  * **Raise  up a signal flag for the nations** - This means God is calling the people of the other nations to come to the land of Israel.
