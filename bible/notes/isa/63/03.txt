===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **I have trod grapes** - Here "I" refers to Yahweh. This imagery refers to Yahweh destroying his enemies. (See: [[:aa:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) 
    * **For I looked forward to the day of vengeance** - Here "day" refers to a period of time. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
