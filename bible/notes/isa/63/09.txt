===== Translation Notes: =====
  * **Through all their suffering** - Here "their" refers to the people of Israel. Isaiah included himself as member of the people. AT: "Through all our suffering"
  * **he suffered too** - Here "he" refers to Yahweh.
  * **the messenger of his presence** - This is a messenger or angel that represents the presence of God.
  * **he lifted them up and carried them** - This refers to when God protected and saved the people of Israel from the Egyptians many years earlier. (See: [[:aa:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:aa:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
