===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **Ho** - This word has a different use here than it did in [[en:bible:notes:zec:02:06|2:6]]. This word is used to describe a joyful shout.
  * **Daughter of Zion**  - This is another name for Jerusalem that refers to the city like a daughter of the heavenly city of Zion.
  * **encamp among you**  - The word "encamp" means to set up and use a camp.
  * **nations will join themselves to Yahweh** - "nations will make become loyal to Yahweh"
  * **in that day**  - This phrase refers to a period of time that could be more than a single day. AT: "when this happens"
