Translation Notes:

  * **all flesh** - Yahweh refers to all living creatures by calling them flesh. Flesh is something that is common among all living creatures. (See: [[:aa:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
  * **been roused out of the heavens** - This phrase refers to Yahweh being caused to take action on earth.
  * **roused** - to be woken up or made more alert.
