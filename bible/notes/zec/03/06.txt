===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **solemnly commanded Joshua** - "commanded Joshua in a very serious manner" 
  * **will walk in my ways**  - Walking in the same path refers to doing the same things. (See: [[:aa:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
  * **if you will keep my commandments**  - Obeying God's commandments is like a person keeping them and not losing them.  "if you will remember and obey my laws" (See: [[:aa:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
  * **adjudicate** "make judgements"
  * **keep my courts** - "be responsible for"
