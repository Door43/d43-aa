===== Translation Notes: =====


    * **fifteenth day in the eighth month**  - "15th day in the 8th month" or "day number 15 in month number 8" (See: Translate Numbers)
    * **in the month when he had planned in his own mind**  - Here "in his own mind" means that the idea and plan to ordain a feast came from Jeroboam. AT: "in the month that he chose" (See: Synecdoche)
