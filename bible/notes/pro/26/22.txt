===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **They go down to the inner parts of the body **- Here the "body" may refer to abdomen or belly. The gossips wound a person physically. This sentence is equivalent to Proverbs 18:8. (See: Simile)
  * **The glaze overlaying an earthern vessel is like burning lips and an evil heart **- AT: "An earthern vessel covered with silver edges is like shining, smooth lips and an evil heart." Silver glossing refers to the glazing process of a base pot. (See: Simile)
