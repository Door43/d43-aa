===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **It is better to live in the desert **- the idea of desert posits a mental image. (See: [[:aa:ta:vol2:translate:figs_apostrophe]])
  * **a woman who stirs up strife and complains too much **- such quarrel came from anger. (See: [[:aa:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
  * **but a foolish man wastes it **- AT: "but a foolish man spends it up" or "but a foolish man swallows it up."
